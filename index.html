About Me

Hello! It's Jeba, an Assistant Software Engineer(L-II) at Dynamic Solution Innovators Ltd.(DSi). My expertise spans from automating workflows with scripting languages like Bash, Perl, and Python to optimize data-driven output using proprietary software like Papyrus Designer. I pursued my B.Sc. degree in Computer Science & Engineering from Rajshahi University of Engineering & Techonology(RUET). During my undergraduate years, I have participated in several inter-university and national programming contests, where I honed my problem-solving skills using C++. Through these experiences, I developed a strong foundation in algorithmic thinking and object-oriented programming (OOP), enabling me to tackle complex challenges efficiently.
I'm a machine learning enthusiast as well. I've recently attended HerWILL AI for Equality 2024 Workshop. Additionally, I'm the first author of the paper Transfer Learning Based Approaches for Classification of Chest CT Scans: Normal, COVID-19, and Pneumonia published in 2024 6th International Conference on Electrical Engineering and Information & Communication Technology (ICEEICT), IEEE, 2024, pp. 841–846. This was my undergraduate thesis topic. I also took part in Ada Lovelace Datathon 2021.
Moreover, I developed a couple of user-friendly native applications using Flutter, designed for academic and educational purposes. In addition, I'm proficient in web development and eager to continue expanding my knowledge. As a versatile learner, I'm dedicated to make a significant contribution to organizational and personal growth.
